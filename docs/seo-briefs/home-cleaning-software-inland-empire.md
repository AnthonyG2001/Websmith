## 2. Search intent classification
- **Primary intent:** Commercial (0.718 on the search-intent endpoint for `seo for cleaning companies`). Sister term `cleaning business marketing` at 0.81 commercial, `maid service marketing` at 0.843 commercial. Cleanly commercial across the cluster — no mixed intent.
- **User goal in plain English:** A residential or commercial cleaning business owner (often a maid-service operator with 2-10 cleaners, or a commercial janitorial operator) is researching SEO help. **Cleaning has the highest commercial intent of any S3 vertical** (0.843 for maid services) because the buyer pool is large and competitive — there are far more cleaning businesses per capita than chimney sweeps or garage door installers.
- **Decision-stage:** Mid-consideration — actively shopping. The buyer has already decided to spend on marketing; they're comparing approaches and providers.
- **Implication:** Page must address **the bifurcated market** — residential maid services (high-volume, lower-ticket, recurring) vs. commercial janitorial (lower-volume, higher-ticket, longer sales cycle). Don't try to be both at once; lead with residential and footnote commercial.
