What does local SEO actually do for a Highland business?
Local SEO is the work that puts your Google Business Profile in the Maps 3-pack when someone in Highland searches for what you sell. It's the combination of GBP optimization, citation consistency, review cadence, and on-site schema markup. For most service businesses in Highland the 3-pack is where 60 to 80 percent of inbound phone calls originate. If you're not in it, you're invisible to the buyer with intent right now.
Why is local SEO competitive in Highland?
The Highland pack is unusual. Our May 13, 2026 5×5 grid scan returned 39 unique competing GBPs with 17 actively winning grid points — but the raw count is inflated. STEELGRADE owns 96% share of local voice. Below that, the next ten 'competitors' are mostly Primo Water Refill GBPs (a bottled-water supplier whose store listings miscategorized into the local-SEO pack). The genuine operators are Vivid Recall Media (32% SoLV), SK Marketing Co (28%), Digital camp&pub marketing (20%), and Marketing 720 from Redlands (12%). For a Highland business, the real competitive set is thinner than the raw count suggests.
Do I need a new website or can you work with my existing one?
Both work. The Standard plan layers GBP, schema markup, and on-page improvements onto your current site — works well if the site is under 5 years old and built on a modern CMS. The Flagship plan rebuilds the site from scratch as part of the engagement — better if your current site is slow, hard to edit, or not mobile-friendly. We run a site audit in week one and tell you which fits.
How quickly will I see results in Highland?

The Primo Water Refill pollution in the Highland pack actually helps a serious operator — once the algorithm tightens or reviewers report the miscategorizations, the visible pack thins.
