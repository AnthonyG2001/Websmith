[03]  QUESTIONS BEFORE YOU REACH OUT

Reaching out, answered straight.

Which cities do you actually serve outside of Redlands?
We work with service-business owners across the Inland Empire: Redlands, San Bernardino, Riverside, Ontario, Fontana, Rancho Cucamonga, and roughly fifteen other cities in San Bernardino and Riverside counties. Our office is in Redlands. We don't take on clients outside the IE because local SEO benefits from knowing the neighborhoods, HOAs, and commuter patterns of the area you're competing in — and we'd rather be right about one region than vague about the country.
How quickly will I hear back after I send the form or call?
The same business day. Every inquiry — form submission, phone call, or email — gets a real human reply by end of business on the day we receive it. If you contact us over the weekend, you'll hear back the next business day. We don't use auto-responders or chatbots for the first reply. The person who reads your message is the person you'll be working with if we end up engaging.
What's actually in the free 30-day GBP audit?
A hand-written review of your Google Business Profile and the geo-grid around your service area. We pull a Local Falcon scan of your primary keyword, check your GBP categories, service-area definition, review velocity, photo recency, and citation consistency across the major directories. You get a written summary of what's working, what's broken, and what to fix first. No sales pitch wrapped around it — if the audit is enough for you to fix things yourself, that's a fine outcome.
What happens after I submit the contact form?
You get an email confirmation immediately, then a personal reply from one of us the same business day. The first reply usually asks a few clarifying questions (your service area, your top three competitor names, whether you already have a GBP claimed). From there we either book a 20-minute call or send the free audit directly, depending on what makes more sense. No automated funnel — we read every message ourselves.
What should I have ready before I contact you?
Three things help us reply with something useful instead of generic. One: your business name and the city your GBP is registered in, so we can pull your current rankings before we respond. Two: the service area you actually cover (some owners list a city but work three over). Three: whether you've worked with an SEO agency before and what went wrong if so. None of these are required — you can also just send a URL and we'll go from there.
