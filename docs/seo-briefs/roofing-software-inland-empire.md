## 2. Search intent classification
- **Primary intent:** Commercial (0.526 commercial + 0.394 informational on the search-intent endpoint; DFS labs concurs `main_intent: commercial`). Slightly more commercial than Plumbing's 0.462.
- **User goal in plain English:** A roofing business owner (often after a slow summer or before storm season) is researching SEO agencies while also learning how SEO for roofing works. Roofers are uniquely sophisticated about lead-quality (vs. lead-volume) because storm-damage chasers and insurance-restoration leads dominate the vertical.
- **Decision-stage:** Consideration (mid-funnel) — vetting *who*, while seeking pricing transparency and proof of seasonal-demand competence.
- **Implication:** Lead with the seasonal-demand framing (storm-season prep) and prove specificity through GBP-category knowledge (Roofing Contractor / Roof Repair Service). Slightly heavier CTA emphasis than Plumbing because commercial probability is higher.
