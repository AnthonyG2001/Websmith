## 4. §04 deep-dive — the Ontario review paradox

**The visceral hook.**

Scan key: `5b6bbd9d742e7dd`. 5×5 grid, 2-mi radius from Ontario, keyword `plumber [city]`.

| Rank | GBP | SoLV | Google reviews |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Advanced Gen Plumbing | 72% | **71** |
| 2 | Roto-Rooter | 60% | 1,409 |
| 3 | All Pro Plumbing | 40% | **8,080** |

All Pro Plumbing has **8,080 Google reviews**. Roto-Rooter is the national brand with 1,409. Both sit behind a **71-review** independent operator.

### Three possible explanations

1. **Grid proximity dominates.** Advanced Gen sits closer to the geographic center of the 25-point scan grid. A 71-review GBP in the geometric middle beats an 8,080-review GBP 5 miles off-center.
2. **GBP signal quality**, not raw review count. Advanced Gen may have cleaner category alignment, tighter NAP, better recent-review velocity, or fresher GBP posts.
3. **Pack saturation effect.** Once a competitor crosses ~500 reviews, marginal review count stops contributing meaningfully.

### Implication for any IE plumber

You do not need to outrun an 8,080-review competitor to win the pack. You need to:
- Be physically inside the service-area grid the searcher is in
- Run continuous review velocity (10-20 per month sustained)
- Keep the GBP signal clean — single primary category, NAP parity, weekly posts, photo uploads

### The compounded thesis

Review-bar median ranges from 150 (Fontana) to 1,409 (Ontario/San Bernardino) — a **10× spread**. This is a **fragmented** market.

## 7. Industry-specific deep dive — IE plumbing characteristics

### Emergency-call dominance
Plumbing is the most "emergency" of any IE trade scanned. Pack winners (Anthony's at 100% in Fontana; Ballard at 96% in San Bernardino) all run 24/7 dispatch. Implication: review counts are inflated by "they came at midnight, fixed it, 5-star."

### The multi-trade chain pattern
Three of five top-1 GBPs are multi-trade chains (Plumbing + HVAC + Electrical/AC):
- Redlands #1: Redlands & Henry Bush Plumbing, Heating & AC
- San Bernardino #1: Ballard Plumbing, Heating & AC
- Lasting Legacy in Redlands at #3

A pure-play plumber competing against a multi-trade chain has **3× less review-generation surface area**.

### Water-heater seasonality
October-February IE plumbing search volume peaks on water-heater terms. Single-snapshot methodology does NOT capture — flag in §09.

### Commercial vs. residential
Pack measured is heavily residential because `plumber [city]` is residential-intent.

### The Lasting Legacy outlier
Redlands' #3 is Lasting Legacy Plumbing (1,581 reviews) outranked by an 894-review competitor at #1 and a 917-review at #2. Same Ontario pattern in miniature.

## 10. Honesty constraints

**Banned phrases:**
- "Top plumber in [city]" / "Best plumber" / "Leading plumber"
- "Trusted by [N] plumbers"
- "Industry-leading" / "award-winning"
- Specific own-rank claims for SoftwareStrategists
- "Reply within 1 hour"
- Fabricated case-study metrics

**Required hedging:**
- "Pack leader" not "best in town"
- "Holds X% SoLV across the grid we measured" not "dominates [city] plumbing"
- "Based on the May 12-13 snapshot" prefix on leadership claims
